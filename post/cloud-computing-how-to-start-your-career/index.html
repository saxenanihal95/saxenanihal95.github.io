Cloud Computing: How To Start Your Career?

Written by Puja Majumder on April 23, 2020

Introduction

Do you want to make your career in cloud computing? Yes? Then, you have reached the right place. Undoubtedly, the growth in cloud computing technology has emerged with loads of opportunities for professionals.

However, you must acquire proper skills to make you a component candidate in the world of advanced technology.

It is no denying that nowadays more and more organizations are opting for digital transformations by using the strength of the cloud. Therefore, the demand for IT professionals and team members has raised a lot.

These companies are looking for extracting top business outcomes to ensure a maximum ROI in different types of cloud technology, private, public, and hybrid.

Hence, if you are interested to kickstart your career in this field, keep on reading!

Basic Requirements For Cloud Computing Career

Undoubtedly, every new entrant wants to make a big in his/her profession. The same applies to cloud computing. A successful career in this field lies in cloud leadership. However, it calls for much more than a brilliant technical background.

Moreover, candidates must have more knowledge than on-premise IT strategies. In fact, cloud leadership is looking for the right amalgamation of academic and business skills in candidates. Furthermore, having leadership skills are also mandatory in this regard.

Some Essential Skills For Cloud Computing

Technologies & Platforms:

Are you an experienced IT professional? Then, definitely, you can think about switching to your career in cloud computing.

However, you would still need some additional skills to develop. For example, the journey may seem difficult for you if you do not have any prior experience in one of the public cloud organizations.

However, which of the approaches should you go for? Is it a one-vendor approach or multi-platform one?

The latter one will help you better in acquiring enough experience in today’s job market. Nonetheless, you can still opt for one and start your journey to keep it simple.

Moreover, you must also start thinking of containerization expertise in case you want to be a developer in building as well as running cloud applications.

The latest area of interest in this regard includes Kubernetes and Docker. However, many other alternatives are also there to `start your expertise on.

What Is More On Cloud Computing Career?

On the other hand, automation software is also gaining attention nowadays. Hence, if you would like to work in DevOps, you should start building your basics on Chef, Puppet, Ansible, etc tools.

Since their applications are on the rise, the opportunities are also growing and more and more companies have started searching for employees, who can handle such platforms.

Amalgamation & Multi-Cloud Environments:

Nowadays, different people interpret the cloud differently. Some say it is SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) applications whereas some others opine it to be IaaS (Infrastructure-as-a-Service) platforms.

However, companies in this field need to integrate different cloud vendors and services along with multiple types (private, public, and hybrid). And obviously, as a professional, you must know how to manage such platforms.

Wrapping Up

Definitely, cloud computing holds enormous possibilities and opportunities. And we can hope that it will keep on increasing in the near future.

Hence, it is the right time to start or shift your career to cloud computing.
